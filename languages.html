Languages

These are eather programming launguages I know or I have lots of experience in.

Python (2/10)

Python, a versatile and beginner-friendly programming language, has become a cornerstone in the tech world. Renowned for its readability, simplicity, and extensive libraries, Python is an excellent choice for various applications, from web development and data analysis to artificial intelligence and automation. With a low entry barrier and a vast supportive community, Python offers an accessible introduction to coding, earning it a difficulty level of around 2 out of 10. Whether you're a novice or an experienced developer, Python's elegance and flexibility make it a go-to language for diverse projects.

JavaScript (3/10)

JavaScript, the language of the web, empowers developers to create dynamic and interactive websites. Widely supported by browsers, JavaScript is essential for front-end development, allowing you to bring web pages to life with animations, responsive interfaces, and real-time updates. Its versatility extends to server-side development with Node.js. JavaScript's relatively forgiving syntax and immediate visual feedback make it beginner-friendly, earning it a difficulty level of around 3 out of 10. As a crucial player in modern web development, mastering JavaScript opens doors to a broad range of opportunities and projects.

Swift (4/10)

Swift is a powerful and intuitive programming language developed by Apple for building applications across iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS. Known for its clean syntax and seamless integration with Apple's ecosystem, Swift prioritizes safety, performance, and modern software design principles. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, Swift's readability and concise syntax make it accessible. With a difficulty level of around 4 out of 10, Swift is a great choice for those looking to dive into app development with relative ease.
